What is ShallowTV?

ShallowTV is a media player tool that allows users to play M3U playlists and stream media from URLs that users provide themselves. The application functions solely as a playback interface and does not include, bundle, or distribute any media content, streaming links, or playlists.

Think of ShallowTV like a web browser or media player - it is simply a tool that can access content from URLs you provide. The application itself contains no content.

Privacy - Data We Collect

ShallowTV is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect personal identification information such as your name, email, or account details. However, we use the following third-party services to help improve the app:

Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app in aggregate. This service collects:

  • Usage Data: App opens, screen views, and feature interactions
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Anonymous Identifiers: A random app-instance identifier (not linked to your personal identity)

This data is used solely for analytics purposes to improve the app experience. It is not linked to your identity and is not used for advertising or tracking across apps.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix app crashes. When a crash occurs, Crashlytics collects:

  • Crash Logs: Stack traces and error information
  • Device State: Device model, OS version, orientation, and available memory at the time of crash
  • App State: App version and the sequence of events leading to the crash

Crash data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. It is not linked to your identity.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Information about the content you access or the URLs you stream
  • The playlists you add to the application
  • Your viewing history or browsing habits

Data Stored Locally

ShallowTV stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Playlist URLs: The M3U playlist URLs you add to the app
  • Media Metadata: Channel names, logos, and categories parsed from your playlists
  • Playback Progress: Your viewing progress for VOD content
  • Favorites: Items you've marked as favorites
  • App Settings: Your preferences and configuration
  • Cached Images: Thumbnails and logos cached for performance

This data remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can delete all data by uninstalling the application.

Network Requests

ShallowTV makes network requests for:

  • Fetching Playlists: From URLs you provide
  • Streaming Content: From stream URLs in your playlists
  • Loading Images: Logos and thumbnails from URLs in your playlists
  • Analytics & Crash Reporting: Anonymous usage and crash data sent to Google Firebase

Playlist, streaming, and image requests go directly to URLs you have added. Analytics and crash data are sent to Google Firebase servers. We do not operate our own data collection servers.
